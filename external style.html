Kavya Maran SRH Owner: In IPL 2024, India finds a new national crush in Kavya Maran, who has captured the hearts of netizens with her infectious charm. During the Sunrisers Hyderabad vs Mumbai Indians match, besides the exhilarating twists and turns on the field, Kavya Maran, the owner of SRH, stole the spotlight with her celebratory dance as her team clinched a victory in their first match of the season. The video of her joyful moment quickly went viral on social media, solidifying her status as India's latest crush.

Kavya Maran rose to prominence in 2018 when she took over ownership of the IPL franchise SunRisers Hyderabad, marking her as a significant figure in the cricketing world. Her father, Kalanithi Maran, is a renowned media mogul and the founder of the Sun Group, further enhancing the family's influential position. Actively involved with SunRisers Hyderabad, Kavya's unwavering support for the team is evident through her presence at all their matches, forging a strong bond with the players.

Estimates peg Kavya Maran's net worth at around USD 50 million (INR 409 crores), according to Jan Bharat Times, underscoring her financial standing within the broader context of the Maran family's wealth. Her father, Kalanithi Maran, previously topped the Tamil Nadu IIFL Wealth Hurun India Rich List 2019 with a net worth of Rs 19,000 crores.
